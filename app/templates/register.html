Create an Account

💡 No sign-up form needed. Because this app uses Auth0 Universal Login, registration happens directly on Auth0's secure hosted page — not here.

Here's how to create your account in 3 clicks:

1

Click "Register via Auth0" below. You'll be redirected to Auth0's secure login page.

2

Choose Sign up with Google (recommended) — or enter an email and password directly on the Auth0 page.

3

Auth0 creates your account, generates your tokens, and redirects you back to your dashboard automatically.

🔒 What gets stored on our server:
Your email, display name, profile picture URL, and encrypted access/refresh tokens. No password is ever stored here — Auth0 and Google handle credential security.
